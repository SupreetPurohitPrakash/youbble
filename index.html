Who We Are

Youbble aspires to be ‘Your bubble’.

You'BBle’s objective is to create a bridge between the cultural challenges of foreign nationals and potential solutions. With You’BBle, initiatives and ideas can thrive more easily within your respective community and beyond.

A small team of visionaries have quietly labored for the past couple of years. This team has been nurturing the concept and doing the hard work of establishing the foundation of You’BBle. Please take a minute and listen to the heart and overview of our vision

Addressing your questions

With You’BBle, you will be able to easily connect with people and businesses that serve, understand, and value your culture, language, and products. You’BBle addresses questions such as:

Get Started
  • Where can I find people that are from my country, understand my language and culture around here?
  • Where can I find food and products that I am used to back home?
  • How can I take my talent, business, service to my community; or collaborate with people from within my community?
  • How can I reach more audiences from within my community and beyond?
  • How can I increase sales of my products?
