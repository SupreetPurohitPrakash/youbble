Youbbler

Youbble has developed our much anticipated digital platform, a free app to connect talents, businesses, freelancers, hobbyists and users within communities tied together by country of origin, common interests, culture, and language to come together and interact in a relevant space. Youbbler is a one-stop shop for all your networking and marketing needs. We bring you closer to your community and your community to you

Youbbler Features

  • i. Users can add friends and network with other users that share common nationality, roots, culture, and language
  • ii. Users can add their businesses, services, and talents to their profile and get more exposure from the Youbble community audience
  • iii. Businesses and Talents can offer promotions, such as deals for users to save and owners to increase sales
  • iv. Businesses and talents can offer to collaborate with others
  • v. Businesses and Talents can showcase their projects through digital media & links
  • vi. Users can “Add to Bucket” (subscribe) businesses and talents of their interest for prompt notifications on any new Project or Promotions being offered

Why use Youbbler?

Here’s the downlow - Our Mission is to cultivate and to share with people of various international communities the essential resources necessary to make their lives more successful. Being immigrants, it was a tough journey for us, and others we met along the way, to get accustomed to different cultures and find our ways around in a foreign land to pave our paths of success. We launched a startup - Youbble and have been dedicating our time and energy in curating a platform for anyone who plans to migrate or travel to other countries, and find helpful resources close to their culture; with an intention to make it easier for people (especially, the next generation of travelers) to explore or settle.
